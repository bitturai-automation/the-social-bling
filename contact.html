Common Questions.

Everything you need to know before booking your strategy call.

How does the strategy call work? +
It's a focused 30-minute session where we deep-dive into your brand, goals, audience, and where you currently stand. We come prepared with observations and leave with a shared vision for what we'll build together. No sales pressure — just honest strategy.
What's the minimum engagement period? +
We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment for social media management and content retainers. This ensures we have enough time to build brand momentum, test what resonates, and start generating compounding results. Project-based work has no minimum.
Do you work with international brands? +
Absolutely. We have clients across India, Dubai, Singapore, the UK, and beyond. Our strategy and content production process is fully remote-ready, and we've delivered global-quality campaigns from day one.
What information should I prepare before the call? +
Come with your brand's current social handles, a rough idea of your goals (followers, sales, brand awareness), your budget range, and 2–3 brands you admire aesthetically. That's it — we handle the rest.
Will I get a dedicated account manager? +
Yes. Every client gets a dedicated strategist and creative point of contact. You won't be passed around or left wondering. Direct communication, fast responses, and full accountability — always.
