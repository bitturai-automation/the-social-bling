FAQ

Questions We Get
Every Day.

Answered honestly — because your time is too valuable for vague replies.

Do you work with startups or only established brands? +
We work with both. Whether you're launching from scratch and need brand positioning + a content strategy, or you're an established brand that needs to scale and modernise your social presence — we have the experience and frameworks to help. What matters to us is ambition, not size.
Do we own the content you create for us? +
Yes — 100%. Once the final deliverables are approved and the project is complete, all intellectual property, raw files, and creative assets belong to you. No licensing fees, no content held hostage. Everything we make for your brand is yours to keep and use however you choose.
Can we hire you for just one service, or is it a bundle? +
You can absolutely engage us for a single service — a brand identity project, a one-time production shoot, or a launch campaign. Many clients start with one service and expand over time. If we believe a combination would serve your goals better, we'll be transparent about it — but we never force bundles.
Is there a minimum contract period for social media management? +
We typically ask for a 3-month minimum for social media management. This isn't to lock you in — it's because meaningful growth takes time. Real results in content and community building don't happen in 4 weeks. Three months gives us enough data and momentum to show you meaningful, compound results.
Do you work with brands outside India? +
Absolutely. We operate across India, Dubai, and Singapore with clients in those markets and beyond. Our team understands the cultural nuances of each region, which means your content won't just be translated — it'll be built natively for your audience's context and platform behaviour.
How involved do we need to be once we hand things over? +
As involved as you'd like to be. Some clients prefer to review and approve every piece of content; others give us creative freedom within agreed guidelines and check in monthly. We work around your schedule. Onboarding involves a one-time brand briefing session, and after that, we can handle everything — or collaborate closely. Your call.
What do you need from us to get started? +
To hit the ground running, we typically need: your logo files and brand kit (if they exist), access to your social media accounts, any existing brand guidelines, a short brand questionnaire we'll send you, and a 30-minute onboarding call. If you don't have brand assets yet, no problem — we'll build them for you as part of onboarding.
